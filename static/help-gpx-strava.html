How to export a GPX file

A GPX file contains your route data. Follow these steps to export one from any Strava activity — it takes about 30 seconds.

1
Open an activity on Strava

Go to strava.com and click on any activity you want to export — a run, ride, or any workout with a route.

2
Click the ••• menu

On the activity page, find the ••• button (next to the pencil icon) and click it.

3
Select Export GPX

From the dropdown, tap Export GPX. The file will download to your device automatically.
